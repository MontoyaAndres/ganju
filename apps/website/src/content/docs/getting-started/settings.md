**Settings** is your organization's control room. A left sub-nav jumps between
each area — **Organization**, **Billing & plan**, **Members**, **Projects**,
**Models**, and the **Danger zone**.

## Organization & billing

The **Organization** section shows when it was created, how many projects and
members it has, and lets you rename it. Just below, **Billing & plan** shows your
current plan and renewal date, plus usage against your allowance — assistant
replies, embedded (RAG) storage, file storage, and projects. Only your
assistant's replies count toward billing; incoming user messages are free.

![The Settings page showing Organization details and the Billing & plan usage breakdown](/images/settings-1.webp)

## Members & projects

Invite teammates to the organization by email — they accept the invitation
in-app — and give them a role. Each **project** has its own member list too, so
you can control exactly who can access which project.

![The Members section and the Projects section, each with an invite-by-email field and roles](/images/settings-2.webp)

## Language models

By default your channels use the system model, but you can bring your own. Under
**Models**, select **Add model**, give it a display name, choose the model (like
Claude Sonnet 4.7), and paste your API key — with an optional base URL and system
prompt. Add it once and reuse it across any channel in the organization.

![The New model form with display name, model, API key, base URL, and system prompt fields](/images/settings-4.webp)

## Danger zone

The **Danger zone** holds destructive, organization-wide actions. **Remove
organization** permanently deletes the organization and everything inside it —
every project, channel, conversation, message, resource, tool, and model. It
can't be undone, so it asks you to confirm.

![The Danger zone with the Remove organization action and its permanent-delete warning](/images/settings-3.webp)
