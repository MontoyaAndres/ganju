Your **organization** represents your team or company — it's where members,
billing, and projects live. Inside it, each **project** is a self-contained
workspace for one assistant, with its own prompts, resources, tools, and
channels.

## Create your organization

The first time you sign in, Ganju asks you to set up an organization and its
first project together. Name your organization, give your project a name and a
short description, then select **Create Organization**.

![The Create Your Organization form, with fields for the organization name, project name, and project description](/images/new-org-project.webp)

## Your project dashboard

You'll land on the project's home. This is where you'll find its **MCP URL** (the
address AI clients connect to), an activity chart, and quick cards for Resources,
Tools, and Prompts.

![The new project dashboard showing the MCP URL, an activity chart, and Resources, Tools, and Prompts cards](/images/new-project.webp)

## Built-in tools

Every project starts with five built-in tools already installed — **List
Resources**, **Read Resource**, **Send Resource**, **Search Resources**, and
**List Prompts** — so your assistant can work with your content right away.
That's the **5** you see on the Tools card. You can add more anytime from the
tools catalog.

![The Tools page listing the five built-in tools installed by default](/images/default-tools.webp)

Invite teammates from your organization settings and assign their roles so the
right people can help you manage the project.
