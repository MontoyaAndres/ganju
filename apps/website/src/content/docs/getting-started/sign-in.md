Ganju is free to start — no credit card required. Sign in at
[app.ganju.ai](https://app.ganju.ai) with your Google or GitHub account, and
you'll land in your dashboard ready to set up your first organization.

![The Ganju sign-in screen, with options to sign in with Google or GitHub](/images/login.webp)

If someone invited you to an existing organization, sign in with the same account
the invite was sent to and you'll join it automatically.
