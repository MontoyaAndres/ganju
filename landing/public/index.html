FAQ

Good questions

Do I need to be technical to use Ganju?

No. If you can fill out a form and click a few buttons, you can set up Ganju — connecting your files, tools, and AI takes just a few minutes, no coding required. (Developers get powerful options too, like connecting their own apps.)

What is MCP?

MCP (the Model Context Protocol) is the standard way AI assistants connect to outside tools and information. You don't need to understand the details — Ganju handles all of it so your AI can safely use your files and apps.

Which AI assistants can I use?

Use Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, or OpenAI (ChatGPT) — just add your own account. Other compatible providers work too.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your passwords and keys are encrypted, never shown in plain text, and never shared. Everything runs on Cloudflare's secure global network — and if you run Ganju yourself, your data stays entirely in your own account.

Can I run Ganju myself?

Yes. Ganju is open source and free to use and modify. Host it on your own account, or let us run it for you on a paid plan.
